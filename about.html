Hi, I'm Delta!

UX Designer & Creative Explorer

Right now, I’m looking for an entry-level UX role. I know I haven’t worked at a company before, but I’m a quick learner, adaptable, and genuinely excited to grow. I’d love the chance to show you what I can do.
